The Official Website for the RMC Robotics Club!

This is our first year of existence EVER!

This year, we are focusing on recruiting members and making sure the team learns the ins-and-outs of robotics. We will be doing everything from advertising to writing code; this season will not be boring.

Will there be competition?

Because we are focusing on building our team this year, we won't be doing any 'official' competition, however we may hold club-based mini-competitions! Stay tuned though, as we are planning to officially compete next year!
